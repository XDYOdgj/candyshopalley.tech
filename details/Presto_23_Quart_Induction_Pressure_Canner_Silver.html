Presto 23-Quart Induction Compatible Pressure Canner

Whether you're a seasoned canner or just starting out, the Presto 23-Quart Induction Pressure Canner is the ultimate choice for reliable and efficient preservation. Its induction-compatible design ensures compatibility with gas, electric, and smooth-top stoves, making it a versatile addition to your kitchen. This canner is built to last, with a heavy-gauge aluminum body and a stainless steel-clad base that delivers even and consistent heat distribution for better results every time.

With a large 23-quart capacity, this unit can handle up to 7 quarts, 20 pints, or 26 half-pints of Mason jars, perfect for canning in bulk. Its dual functionality as a boiling water canner and pressure cooker adds value by enabling you to prepare a wide range of meals with ease. The precise pressure gauge and secure air vent/cover lock system ensure safety and accuracy, giving you confidence throughout the process. Backed by a 12-year warranty, this canner guarantees durability and long-term performance for your pantry.
