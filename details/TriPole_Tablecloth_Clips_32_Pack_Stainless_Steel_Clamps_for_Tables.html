Introducing TriPole Tablecloth Clips

Enhance your table presentation with these durable and reliable clips designed for both indoor and outdoor use. Whether you're hosting a dinner, setting up for a restaurant, or enjoying a picnic, TriPole delivers the perfect solution.

Built to Last

Manufactured from high-quality stainless steel, these tablecloth clips are crafted for long-term use. They resist rust and deformation, ensuring consistent performance even in harsh weather conditions.

Secure & Versatile

With a secure grip, these clips hold tablecloths firmly in place without slipping. They are suitable for tables up to 1.77 inches in thickness and are ideal for a variety of settings, from home to restaurants and beyond.

Easy to Use

Designed for quick installation, these clips can be easily attached and removed with a simple pinch and slide motion. Their seamless design ensures hassle-free handling, making them a practical choice for everyday use.

Perfect for Any Occasion

Whether you're hosting a family dinner at home, setting up for a restaurant service, or preparing for a backyard picnic, these clips are the must-have accessory for any dining setup. Ideal for outdoor events such as picnics, camping, or parties where wind might cause tablecloths to shift.

Choose Your Fit

TriPole offers a variety of package options to suit your needs, making these clips flexible and essential for table cover management. They are especially useful for standard table thicknesses, with a height of 1.7 inches and expandable to 1.77 inches.

Keep Your Table Spotless

These clips help maintain a clean and organized appearance at your tables, ensuring that your tablecloths stay in place and your dining area looks polished and professional. A great choice for casual and formal occasions alike.
